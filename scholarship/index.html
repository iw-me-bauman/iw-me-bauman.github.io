Bauman Scholarship at Bluffton University

The Irwin W. and Mary E. Bauman Scholarship endowed scholarship was established at Bluffton College in about 1980 by John H. Bauman with contributions from Mary K. Bauman Kaufmann and others. It was one of the first ten endowed scholarships at Bluffton. Over the decades, this scholarship has helped make it possible for many students to attend Bluffton University.
